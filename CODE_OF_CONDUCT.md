# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

This project expects professional, respectful collaboration. Good participation
means being direct about technical concerns, assuming good intent, and focusing
feedback on the work.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory
language, sustained disruption, publishing private information, or any conduct
that makes collaboration unsafe.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests,
or other contributions that do not meet these standards. Maintainers may also
temporarily or permanently restrict participation for repeated or severe
violations.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately through the security contact in
`SECURITY.md`. Include relevant links, screenshots, or context when possible.
